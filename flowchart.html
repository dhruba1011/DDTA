What is a Flowchart?

Home Algorithm Flowchart Examples

A flowchart is a diagrammatic representation of an algorithm. It uses symbols like rectangles, diamonds, ovals, and arrows to show the flow of a program.

Common Symbols

  • Terminator (Start/End) – Oval
  • Process – Rectangle
  • Decision – Diamond
  • Arrow – Flow of control
